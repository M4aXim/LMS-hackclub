Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

The Leave Management System (LMS) is represented by Maksim Malbasa and is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our LMS platform.

Both the LMS system and your educational institution (the service provider) value your privacy and are committed to maintaining the confidentiality of your personal information. However, to provide our services effectively, certain data access is necessary and agreed upon during the registration process.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, student ID, class information
  • Leave Request Data: Dates, reasons, supporting documents, approval status
  • Account Information: Login credentials, account preferences, usage history
  • System Data: IP addresses, browser information, device details for security purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • Processing and managing leave requests
  • Communicating with you about your requests
  • Providing customer support and technical assistance
  • Improving our services and user experience
  • Ensuring system security and preventing fraud
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements

4. Data Access and Sharing

Consent for Data Access

By signing up and using our LMS platform, you explicitly agree to grant access to your data to the following parties:

  • Supervisors: Your immediate supervisors will have access to your leave request data for approval and management purposes
  • Directors: School directors will have access to your data, especially for leave requests exceeding 3 days or for administrative oversight
  • Site Administrators: System administrators will have access to all data for technical support, maintenance, and security purposes
  • Developers: The development team (including Maksim Malbasa) may access data for system improvements, bug fixes, and feature development

This consent is mandatory for using our service. Without agreeing to this data access, you cannot use the LMS platform.

5. Data Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols
  • Access Controls: Strict authentication and authorization mechanisms
  • Audit Logging: All data access is logged and monitored
  • Regular Security Audits: Periodic security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Employee Training: Regular privacy and security training for all staff

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for the following periods:

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained while your account remains active
  • Leave Records: Historical leave data is retained for 5 years for compliance and record-keeping
  • Account Deletion: Upon account deletion request, data is retained for 2 years before permanent deletion
  • Audit Logs: System access logs are retained for 3 years for security purposes

7. Your Rights and Choices

Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data (with 60-day written notice)
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a structured format
  • Right to Object: Object to certain types of data processing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent for data access (will affect service availability)

8. Third-Party Services

Our LMS may integrate with third-party services for specific functionalities. These services have their own privacy policies, and we recommend reviewing them. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is designed for educational institutions and may process data of minors. We ensure compliance with applicable laws regarding children's privacy and obtain necessary parental consent where required.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and implement appropriate safeguards.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via email or through the platform. Your continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Information

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your privacy rights, please contact us:

  • Email: lms@maksimmalbasa.in.rs
  • Data Protection Officer: Available through the main contact email
  • Response Time: We will respond to all privacy inquiries within 30 days

12. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: You have explicitly consented to data processing and sharing
  • Contract Performance: Processing is necessary to provide our LMS services
  • Legitimate Interest: Processing for system security and service improvement
  • Legal Obligation: Processing required by applicable laws and regulations

By using our LMS platform, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to this Privacy Policy.
