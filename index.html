About LMS

LMS is a comprehensive leave management solution designed specifically for educational institutions. Our platform bridges the gap between students and teachers, providing a seamless experience for managing leave requests, approvals, and tracking.

Built with security and efficiency in mind, LMS ensures that all leave-related processes are handled professionally while maintaining the highest standards of data protection and user privacy.
