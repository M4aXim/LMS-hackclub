2. Service Description

The LMS is a digital platform designed to facilitate leave request management between students and educational staff (teachers and directors) in educational institutions. The service allows users to submit, track, and manage leave requests through a secure online interface.

3. User Responsibilities

  • You must provide accurate and truthful information when submitting leave requests
  • You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials
  • You must not use the service for any unlawful or unauthorized purpose
  • You must comply with all applicable laws and regulations

4. Data Sharing and Consent

Explicit Consent for Data Sharing

By submitting a leave request through our platform, you explicitly consent to the sharing of your personal data with the following parties:

  • Class Teachers: Your leave request data will be shared with your designated class teacher for review and approval purposes
  • School Directors: For leave requests exceeding 3 days, your data will also be shared with school directors for additional approval
  • Administrative Staff: Relevant administrative personnel may access your data for record-keeping and compliance purposes

This consent is mandatory for the processing of your leave request. Without this consent, we cannot process your leave application.

5. Administrative Access and Data Collection

System Administration and Diagnostics

System administrators have access to all data stored within the LMS platform for the following purposes:

  • Technical Diagnostics: To identify and resolve system issues, bugs, and performance problems
  • Security Monitoring: To detect and prevent unauthorized access, fraud, or security breaches
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve system functionality
  • Compliance Auditing: To ensure adherence to legal and regulatory requirements
  • Data Backup and Recovery: To maintain system integrity and data availability

Administrative access is strictly controlled and logged. All administrative actions are monitored and audited for security purposes.

6. GDPR Compliance and Data Rights

Your Rights Under GDPR

As a user of our service, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: You can object to certain types of data processing

7. Data Deletion Policy

60-Day Written Notice Requirement

In accordance with GDPR requirements, users must provide 60 days written notice before data deletion:

  • Written Notice Required: You must submit a written deletion request via email to lms@maksimmalbasa.in.rs
  • 60-Day Processing Period: We require 60 days from receipt of your written notice to ensure complete data deletion
  • Comprehensive Deletion: During this period, we will systematically remove your data from all systems, databases, and backups
  • Verification Process: Our team will verify that all your personal data has been completely deleted
  • Confirmation: We will provide written confirmation once deletion is complete and verified
  • Withdrawal Period: You may withdraw your deletion request within 30 days of submission

Important: The 60-day period allows us to ensure thorough deletion from all systems, including backups, audit logs, and any integrated third-party services. This timeframe is necessary to comply with GDPR requirements for complete data erasure.

Note: Some data may be retained longer if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., financial records, audit trails).

8. Data Security
